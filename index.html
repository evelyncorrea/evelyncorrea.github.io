About Me

Currently living in Rio de Janeiro, I was graduated in Digital Games Development by NAVE/Colégio Estadual José Leite Lopes, located in Tijuca, Rio de Janeiro.

Co-founder of the startup Hackgirls, that works to encourage girls and women to get in the tech market through the game development.

I have experience with game and app development with Unity, Android Studio, Visual Studio and Construct2 and with C#, Java and HTML5 languages.

I am familiar with Scrum methodology and with management and versioning tools such as Trello and GitHub.

Besides programming, I also have experience with Adobe softwares such as Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere and InDesign.
